## Key Recovery Terms

- **MTPD:** Maximum tolerable period of disruption
- **RTO:** Target time to restore a service
- **RPO:** Maximum acceptable data loss measured in time
- **WRT:** Time needed to validate, reconcile, and resume work after technical recovery

Recovery objectives should be approved by business owners and supported by tested capabilities.

## Human Safety Comes First

During a major disruption, organizations should prioritize:

- Human life and safety
- Emergency communications
- Essential services
- Legal and regulatory obligations
- Protection of sensitive information
- Accurate public communication
- Sustainable workloads for recovery personnel

A recovery action should not create additional danger to employees, customers, communities, or the environment.
