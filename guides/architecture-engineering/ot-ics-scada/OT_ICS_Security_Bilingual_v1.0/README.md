## Safety Comes First

Cybersecurity actions in OT environments may affect:

- Worker safety
- Public safety
- Environmental conditions
- Product quality
- Process stability
- Equipment integrity
- Essential services

Scanning, testing, configuration changes, patching, isolation, and incident-response actions must be authorized and coordinated with operations, engineering, safety personnel, and system owners.

## Disclaimer

This manual is provided for education and general industrial-cybersecurity guidance.

Do not perform active scanning, penetration testing, configuration changes, controller modifications, or network isolation in production OT environments without explicit authorization, safety review, operational coordination, and a tested rollback plan.
