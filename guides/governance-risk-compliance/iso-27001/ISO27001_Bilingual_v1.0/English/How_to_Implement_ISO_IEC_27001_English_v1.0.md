# How to Implement ISO/IEC 27001

A practical guide to building, operating, auditing, and improving an information security management system aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 and Amendment 1:2024.

## Topics

- Context and interested parties
- ISMS scope
- Leadership, policy, and roles
- Risk assessment and treatment
- Statement of Applicability
- Annex A control themes
- Documentation and evidence
- Metrics, internal audit, and management review
- Corrective action and certification readiness
- 30/60/90-day roadmap
