## Risk-Treatment Options

Organizations generally address risk by:

- Avoiding the risky activity
- Reducing likelihood or impact
- Transferring or sharing part of the risk
- Accepting documented residual risk
- Discontinuing unsupported or unsafe systems

Risk acceptance should be approved by an authorized business owner, not only by the person performing the assessment.

## Disclaimer

This manual is provided for education and general cybersecurity guidance. Risk-assessment results depend on the quality of available information, professional judgment, organizational context, legal obligations, and changing threats.

A risk assessment does not guarantee that incidents will not occur.
