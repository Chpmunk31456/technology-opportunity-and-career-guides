# How to Prepare for a SOC 2 Audit

## Cómo prepararse para una auditoría SOC 2

A practical bilingual guide for preparing an organization for a SOC 2 examination, organizing evidence, evaluating controls, correcting gaps, and maintaining audit readiness.

## Topics Covered

- SOC 2 fundamentals
- Trust Services Criteria
- SOC 2 Type 1 and Type 2 examinations
- Organizational scope
- System descriptions
- Control ownership
- Policies and procedures
- Risk assessments
- Evidence collection
- User access reviews
- Change management
- Vendor risk management
- Security awareness
- Incident response
- Business continuity
- Control testing
- Gap remediation
- Management assertions
- Auditor coordination
- Continuous audit readiness
- 30-, 60-, and 90-day preparation plan

## Intended Audience

- Students
- Career changers
- GRC professionals
- Compliance analysts
- Security managers
- Internal auditors
- Technology leaders
- Consultants
- Service organizations
- Small organizations and nonprofits

## Important Notice

Only an appropriately licensed CPA firm can perform a SOC 2 examination and issue a SOC 2 report.

This manual helps organizations prepare for an examination, but it does not replace professional accounting, legal, audit, or compliance advice.
