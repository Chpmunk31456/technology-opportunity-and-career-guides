## Topics Covered

- Privacy governance
- Data inventories and data-flow mapping
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimization
- Transparency and privacy notices
- Data-subject rights
- Retention and secure deletion
- Privacy by design
- Vendor and processor risk
- International data transfers
- AI privacy risks
- Breach response
- Metrics and management review
- 30-, 60-, and 90-day implementation plan
