## Disclaimer

This manual is provided for education and general cybersecurity guidance. Vendor assessments should be proportionate to risk, supported by evidence, and coordinated with security, privacy, legal, procurement, compliance, and business owners.

Organizations should not rely on questionnaires alone when contracts, technical controls, or independent evidence are required.
