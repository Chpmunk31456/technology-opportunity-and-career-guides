## Topics Covered

- SOC mission, scope, and operating model
- Internal, outsourced, co-managed, and virtual SOC models
- SOC roles and responsibilities
- Analyst development and mentoring
- Logging and telemetry strategy
- SIEM and case-management processes
- Detection engineering
- Alert triage and investigation
- Threat intelligence
- Threat hunting
- Incident escalation
- Quality assurance
- Analyst workload and fatigue
- SOC metrics and executive reporting
- 30-, 60-, and 90-day implementation plan

## Intended Audience

- Students
- Career changers
- SOC analysts
- Detection engineers
- Threat hunters
- Incident responders
- Security managers
- Cybersecurity consultants
- Technology leaders
- Small organizations and nonprofits
