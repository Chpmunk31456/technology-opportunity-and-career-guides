## Interview Principles

- Be honest about your experience
- Do not invent certifications or projects
- Use specific examples
- Explain your reasoning clearly
- Admit when you do not know something
- Show how you would find the answer
- Connect technical decisions to business risk
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Treat every interview as a professional conversation
