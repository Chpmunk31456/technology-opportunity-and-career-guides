# Technology Opportunity and Career Guides

Free global resources helping people move from learning to opportunity.

This project connects people with:

- Employment opportunities
- Internships
- Apprenticeships
- Fellowships
- Mentorship programs
- Graduate and trainee programs
- Free educational resources
- Technology career guides
- Portfolio and GitHub guidance
- Resume and interview preparation
- Accessibility and accommodation resources
- Job-scam prevention

## Mission

Many people have the ability to make a meaningful difference in the world, but they lack money, guidance, accommodations, connections, or access to reliable information.

This project exists to help at least one person use technology to change the world for the better—and to encourage that person to help someone else.

## Project Roadmap

### Step 1: Global Opportunities

Verified employment, internship, apprenticeship, fellowship, mentorship, traineeship, graduate-program, and returnship opportunities.

### Step 2: Free Education

Free or genuinely affordable courses, laboratories, tools, portfolio projects, and certification pathways.

### Step 3: Career Guides

Clear pathways into technology, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, cloud computing, data, software development, accessibility, GRC, privacy, and emerging careers.

## Community Commitment

We believe another person's success does not reduce our own.

We encourage everyone who benefits from this project to help one other person by sharing an opportunity, explaining a requirement, reviewing a project, translating a resource, or offering encouragement.

**Help one person. Share one verified opportunity. Teach one useful skill. Leave the path clearer than you found it.**
